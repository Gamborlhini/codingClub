Mission Statement

Hawken Coding Club is dedicated to the inclusive and accessible exploration of computer science in web and app design, machine learning, game development and more.

Whether you're an absolute beginner or you're an advanced programmer, Hawken Coding Club has resources that will allow you to advance in computer science. If you join, we will provide the resources for you to grow and will recommend you to certain sources, but will never force anything upon you. Your advancement is entirely at your pace so why not give Hawken Coding Club a try? Browse through our website if you want to learn more.
